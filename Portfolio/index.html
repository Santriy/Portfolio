Intro

Hi! My name is Santeri Yliniemelä, and I am a passionate game development student with experience in programming, level design, and Unity game development.

Since 2012, I have been designing levels as a hobby. I started with HPL2 engine used in Amnesia The Dark Descent, creating some levels that showcase my creativity and attention to detail.

In 2020, I started programming as a hobby with little to no prior experience. Despite this, I quickly discovered a talent for coding and have since honed my skills through personal projects and my work in a small software company.

My passion for game development began when I was a child, and I have always dreamed of working in the game industry. I believe that gaming has the power to connect people and change lives, and I am excited to be a part of an industry that is constantly evolving and pushing the boundaries of what is possible.

In this portfolio, you will find examples of my level design work, programming projects, and Unity game development. Each project showcases my ability to bring ideas to life and my commitment to delivering high-quality work.

Thank you for taking the time to visit my portfolio. I am eager to explore new opportunities in the game industry and continue to grow my skills and knowledge.

Aetherfall

Aetherfall is a first-person puzzle platformer set in a dystopian world where the rich control the upper districts and the less fortunate live beneath them. It was developed in 4 days by me and my partner for Finnish College Jam #4, it was one of around 30 entries and it won the first place and an "Honorable Mention" price sponsored by Yahaha Studios. The theme set by the organisers was "Aetherpunk", and we decided to also include "Unstable" and "Time Manipulation" which were a few optional diversifiers they provided us with.

The idea for this project came when I booted up Unity and found a quick character controller from the asset store, it had a small feature in it that changed the player gravity 90 degrees when triggered.

In Aetherfall, the player controls a member of the local resistance, trying to find their way into the upper districts after being handed an artifact that allows them to use Aether, the substance that is the energy source of everything in their world.

The bigger idea behind this project was that the main goal of this game is to navigate the lower districts of the city, using the artifact and the fact that it allows you to see and interact with unstable gravity wells to slowly climb to the upper districts to find the Syndicate that's responsible for the oppression of the less fortunate.

There were many ideas that me and my partner wanted to introduce in this project, but ultimately had to scrap due to the 96 hour time limit, especially since we both have our day jobs.
